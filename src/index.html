Need help applying for PIP or ESA?

C-App is designed to help people with disabilities or long term physical or mental health conditions to apply for two benefits, Personal Independence Payment (PIP) and Employment and Support Allowance (ESA).

You may have been contacted by the Department for Work and Pensions about making a claim for PIP or ESA, or moving on to PIP or ESA, you may be facing a reassessment, you may yourself have decided to make an application, or you may just want to find out more.

Whatever the reason you’re here, c-App can help.

We understand that the benefits system can be confusing, and applying for benefits overwhelming. This site walks you through how to apply for ESA or PIP and the assessment process involved so you’ll know what to expect.

The site also lets you try answering the sorts of questions that get asked in the assessment processes, and get a sense of the points you will need to get across.

If you’re of working age, and can’t work because of illness or disability, you could be eligible for ESA.

If you have a long-term health condition or a disability and are aged 16 to 64 you could be eligible for PIP to help you meet the extra costs you face.
